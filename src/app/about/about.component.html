What is
WADU?

WADU, in short, is an assistance platform. WADU helps college students of ages 18 to 30 years learn what type of jobs are available by the type of hobbies, skills or skills yet to be learned the person has.
WADU does not focus on minumum wage, or obscene jobs.


Why make
WADU?

There is a growing number of technological advances and innovation in the world. With this huge flow of information and technology, it is no surprise that their is a growing uncertainty amongst the people of today in terms of choosing a career. WADU solves that problem by giving the people options of what type of jobs are out there. The main focus is to help people do what they love. A friend of mine once said, "I really love movies and love screen writing and I couldn't imagine myself without both."
